\# Prompt Iteration History



\## Initial Version



\*\*What changed:\*\* Output was inconsistent — sometimes a paragraph, sometimes bullets, sometimes very long. No control over format or length.



\---



\## Revision 1



\*\*What changed:\*\* Added explicit format instruction (3 bullet points). Output became more consistent and concise. However, bullet points were sometimes too brief and lost important context.



\---



\## Revision 2



\*\*What improved:\*\* Adding a system role and specifying that each bullet should be a complete sentence improved output quality significantly. Summaries were more informative and consistently structured. Length stayed appropriate across different input sizes.
